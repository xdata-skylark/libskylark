Random Fourier Transform

Sketch transform into Eucledian space of fuctions in an RKHS implicitly defined by a vector and a shift-invariant kernel.

See: Ali Rahimi and Benjamin Recht Random Features for Large-Scale Kernel Machines NIPS 2007.
